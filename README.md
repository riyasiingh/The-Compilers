# The-Compilers

🛺 Autorickshaw Odyssey
"A city that never repeats itself."
🏙️ The Vision
Cities are not predictable machines; they are ecosystems. Traffic pulses like blood through narrow roads. Vendors appear where there was space seconds ago. Rain arrives uninvited. Street dogs wander into lanes. Construction barricades grow like temporary mountains.

In Autorickshaw Odyssey, you aren't just playing a level—you are surviving a living Mumbai traffic simulation. The road evolves as you drive, demanding sharper reflexes and better "survival instincts" as the quiet village outskirts transform into a chaotic, rain-slicked metropolis.

🚀 Key Features
🛠️ Progressive City Evolution
The simulation undergoes a cellular shift every 2000 distance units. The environment doesn't just loop; it evolves:

Village Phase: Sparse trees, farms, and lower speeds.

Suburb Phase: Mixed shops, roadside buildings, and increasing traffic. ( Will be added )

City Phase: Dense concrete, heavy construction, and maximum chaos.

🧠 Advanced Survival Logic
Dynamic Scaling: Speed ramps from 4 to 12, and obstacle density triples as you progress.

Ecosystem Obstacles: Features "Street Dog AI" with wandering movement, construction barricades, vendor stalls, and unpredictable bikes.

Survival Instincts: Reach a distance of 4000 to unlock "Quick Dodge," providing faster lane-switching capabilities to handle high-speed traffic.

Weather Interference: At distance 5000, random monsoon rain arrives, adding a blue atmospheric overlay and slicker control physics.

🍢 Authentic Mumbai Artifacts
Survival comes with luck. Collect scattered items to boost your score:

Chai Glasses: Traditional brown cutting-chai cups.

Snacks: Hot pakoras and street-side fritters.

Coins: Spinning gold rewards found amidst the chaos.

🎨 High-Fidelity Visuals
Realistic Rickshaw: A detailed yellow-and-black sprite with custom roof, headlights, and wheels.

Physics-Based Tilt: The rickshaw leans into turns for a tactile driving feel.

Side-View Ecosystem: Parallax trees, buildings of varying heights, and roadside tea stalls that stay locked to the ground for a static, realistic feel.

📝 Roadmap & Planned Updates
🎨 Visual & Contrast Polish (Next Round)
UI Contrast Refinement: Some current colors are highly contrasting. In the next iteration, I will adjust the UI text shadows and background saturation to ensure perfect readability across all three phases (Village/Suburb/City).

Sprite Balancing: Ensuring all objects maintain a consistent aesthetic as the speed increases.

⚙️ Feature Suggestions
Fuel System: A petrol meter that requires you to collect fuel cans to prevent the simulation from ending.

Day/Night Cycle: Transitioning from afternoon sun to a neon-lit city night with active headlights.

Contextual Audio: Integrating a "Horn" mechanic that sounds automatically during close-call dodges.

Pedestrian AI: Adding slow-moving vendor carts and pedestrians near the suburb shops to thicken the "ecosystem" feel.

🕹️ Controls
Arrow Left / Right: Navigate lanes and dodge obstacles.

Goal: Survive as long as possible, collect artifacts, and evolve through all city phases.

🛠️ Technical Implementation
Smooth Chaos: Built with requestAnimationFrame at 60fps for lag-free performance.
Memory Management: Optimized array filtering to clean up off-screen objects and maintain high performance.
